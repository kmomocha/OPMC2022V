Orthotics and Prosthetics Medical Centre,Indore

The Orthotics Prosthetics Research & Rehabilitation Clinic is one of the leading providers of high quality orthotic and prosthetic patient care services and products. The "OPRRC"was founded by certified Orthotist and Prosthetist Dr.Momocha Kshetrimayum who was dissatisfied with large impersonal corporations where the constant pressures of consolidation and cost containment can hamper effective patient care. He wanted to make a truly difference and established a practice based upon his philosophy and management protocol.

The clinic represents proud traditional values of quality,integrity,dedication,and value for service rendered to the patients. These ideals, formed the basis for the Orthotics Prosthetics Research & Rehabilitation Clinic, and strategy of "putting quality, satisfaction and care first". This is not a marketing slogan, but a fundamental article of faith which connects the center to the boundless opportunities available to an entrepreneur dedicated to finding solutions to human needs. The clinic offer the best of both world--the professional management and information systems with the caring, personal service. These quality system with our traditional emphasis on quality and service assure the referring surgeons and physicians, that exemplary levels of patient care is achieved. Our success is the result of the collective efforts of management and all employees sharing the decision making process and assuming personal responsibility for the integrity of the work.
